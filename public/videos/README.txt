# Video Assets

For the "About" section, please place a video file named `carpentry-art.mp4` in this directory.

Recommended specs:
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1080p or 720p
- Orientation: Portrait or Square works best, but Landscape will crop (object-cover).
- Content: A close-up of a carpenter planing wood, sanding, or carving details.
- Size: Keep it under 5MB for performance.
